My Process Overview
You’re part of the process.
I believe in full collaboration and sharing as part of the process.
Kickoff Meeting

I start by having a meeting, so we get a better understanding of your business needs.

I'll set goals for the new website and define the scope of the project, i.e., what web pages and features the site require to fulfil the goals and the timeline for building those out.

content strategy

I work with you on getting the assets together, like photos of your client work, projects your team has worked on, and then I build a content strategy.
‍
(content strategy is the process of translating business objectives and goals into a plan that uses content as a primary means of achieving those goals)

Design & develop

I start the design process by defining the visual direction and layout.
After that, I conduct a series of interviews with you to establish a visual language according to your business needs.
‍
Based on this information, our designer will create mockups and any other graphics required for the visual language, Also I create a reference guide and comprehensive style for the project, which allows us to increase the speed of development and maintain visual consistency.

Test & launch

During the launch phase, we ready your new website to go live to the world.

Part of that process includes handle the hosting, connecting the new website to your domain, and one final test to make sure everything is perfect.

Our partnership doesn’t end once the website goes live: I am here to support you with technical, marketing, and strategy support.
